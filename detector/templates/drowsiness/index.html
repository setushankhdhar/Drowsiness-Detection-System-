Process

How It Works

Three steps from camera to alert — all running locally on your device in real time.

01

Face Detection

OpenCV's facial landmark model locates your face and isolates your eye regions every frame — even in low light.

02

EAR Calculation

The Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) measures how open your eyes are. A sustained drop below the threshold flags drowsiness.

03

Instant Alert

A loud alarm fires immediately when drowsiness or yawning is detected — and again when your eyes reopen.

Detection States

Three Alert Levels

The system continuously tracks your state and responds appropriately.

✓

Active

Eyes open, alert, and fully detected. No action needed — monitoring continues silently.

⚠

Yawning

Mouth aspect ratio exceeds threshold. Early warning state — alarm sounds to keep you alert.

!

Drowsy

EAR drops below threshold for consecutive frames. Critical state — flashing alarm triggers immediately.
